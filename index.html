Emmanuel College Chapel entrance view looking at the alter.

Emmanuel College is a Catholic college formed by the Sisters of Notre Dame in 1919

The college resides in Boston, MA. About a block from Fenway Park.

Emmanuel College is home to about 2,000 undergraduates from all over the country and world.

All students are required to take two religion courses. One in the Christian tradition and one in any tradition they would like.

All of the athletes at Emmanuel College are blessed in this chapel at the beginning of their season.

The current serving president of Emmanuel College is a Sister of Notre Dame, Sister Janet Eisner.

Emmanuel College this year will be celebrating its 100 years.
